- "95% confidence interval: 62%-72%"

**Confident Authority:**
- "Our data definitively shows..."

**Gracious Positioning:**
- "Thanks to category leaders like Searchable..."

### Don'ts ‚ùå

- ‚ùå "Get your free audit now!"

**Avoid Speculative Claims:**
- ‚ùå "AI will replace SEO..."
- ‚ùå "The future of search is..."
- ‚ùå "Everyone needs this..."

**Avoid Unsupported Comparisons:**
- ‚ùå "Better than competitors..."
